Past Events
Webflow Masterclass #1
at Flying Saints Design Studio, Delhi

The Flying Saints Studio called upon peoplewho.design team for an exclusive Webflow masterclass. It was a 3 day workshop in which we introduced the team to the tool, built webpages together and made complex web interactions visually in webflow.

Webflow Masterclass #2
at Loconav, Gurgaon

We had our second webflow meetup in Gurgaon at the Loconav HQ. People showed up on a very short notice and "had the experience of a lifetime", said someone on the feedback form :)
People from non-tech background found the workshop exceptionally helpful, which has inspired us to reach out to more and more folks from non-tech departments.

Delhi Design Meetup
at Loconav, Gurgaon

This event was in association with Angelhack India. This was a new experience as a few of the best people in the industry showed up to share their experiences. From learning to design our lives to become better humans, to reducing the friction between developers and designers, we had an awesome experience. Delhi Webflow Meetup | Build Stunning Websites Without Code
at Kite HQ, Delhi

It was a roller coaster ride at the Webflow meetup at KiteHQ. We changed the meetup location thrice in the same building as things kept breaking one after the other. The team moved chairs up and down the floors, and finally to the basement three storeys down. But when the people showed up, all seemed well, and we had a great time learning new things.
